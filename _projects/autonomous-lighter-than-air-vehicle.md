Participated in the Defend the Republic autonomous drone competition at Lehigh University.

Developed a lightweight flying vehicle using helium gas buoyancy, with responsibilities for the main body and object-grabbing device. The build used carbon pipes, balsa wood, 3D-printed parts, small BLDC motors, carbon rods, and vinyl film.
