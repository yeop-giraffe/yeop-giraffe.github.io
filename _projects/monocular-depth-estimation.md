Worked on monocular depth estimation training methods at Drexel University's Intelligent Machine Perception and Learning Lab.

The project used CycleGAN to convert simulation data into images resembling real-world environments, then evaluated predicted depth against ground-truth LiDAR point clouds. The mixed real and converted synthetic dataset improved supervised monocular depth estimation performance.
