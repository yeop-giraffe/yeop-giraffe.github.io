Developed a drone control assistance HMI system to reduce crashes caused by human error during indoor drone flights.

The system used ROS on a Jetson Nano companion computer to send flight commands to a Pixhawk flight controller. Communication was built with MAVLink and MAVROS, and flight-assist algorithms were tested in ROS and Gazebo simulation.
